Easy Oven Roasted Asparagus

This asparagus recipe is super easy and works as a side dish for most dishes! The original recipe is from allrecipes.com, but is presented here without weird ads, auto-playing videos, and mysteriously uncloseable drop down menus.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch thin asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Mix olive oil and asparagus together in a bowl. Then add and mix in parmesean, garlic, salt, and pepper
  3. Bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until just tender
  4. Sprinkle with lemon juice before serving

Pro Tip!

If you have heart problems or are looking for a healthier option, use less oil and salt! There's a lot in this recipe and it may be too salty for some. But, as celebrity chef Gordon Ramsey often says, "Oil and salt make everything taste good, even my own turds"
